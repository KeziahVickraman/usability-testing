Help & FAQ

Enter your departure city, destination, travel dates, and cabin class in the booking widget, then click "Search Flights". You can use the X button on any field to clear and re-enter your selection.
Baggage allowance depends on your cabin class and destination. Economy allows 25–30 kg checked baggage; Business Class allows 40 kg; First Class and Suites allow 50 kg. Visit the Baggage Information page for full details.
KrisFlyer Miles are earned on eligible Singapore Airlines, SilkAir, and partner airline flights, as well as partner hotels, car rentals, and credit card spending. Miles can be redeemed for free flights, seat upgrades, hotel stays, and more. Join KrisFlyer free.
Yes — go to "Manage" in the navigation to view, change, or cancel your booking. Fare conditions vary by ticket type. Flexible and full-fare tickets allow free changes; promotional fares may have fees.
